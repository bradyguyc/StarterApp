MyNextBook


This app integrates with openlibrary.org and enhances the experience for tracking your reading status of book series.

Want to know what is the next book to read in your series.
Want to be notified when a new book in your series is available.
Just want to keep track of all your reading.

This app works in conjunction with openlibrary.org to do just that.

Getting Started



You got to this page in this app by installing and creating an account with MyNextBook. This account is your MyNextBook account and is used for data and features not provided by openlibrary.org.


In order to use this app you will need an openlibrary.org account and will need to provide your login credentials to openlibrary.org to this app.  If you already use openlibrary.org this is your username and password.  If not you will need to create an openlibrary.org account and use those credenitals.   In the settings page of the app provide your openlibrary.org credentials, test the connection and then head back to the home page.


Your credentials are saved in an encrypted format on your device only by the app.
